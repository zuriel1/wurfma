Warm Chocolate and Raspberry Tart, Raw Cream.


INGREDIENTS

For the pastry:
225g Plain Flour
110g Butter
80g Sugar
1 Large Egg.
1 Punnet Raspberries - Perry Court Farm
200g Dark chocolate
100g Butter
4 Whole Eggs
60g Caster sugar
150g flour
Raw cream

STEPS


1. Rub flour, butter and sugar together and bind with egg to make pastry. Leave to set in fridge for 30 minutes, Roll and line a 7inch tart case. Blind bake, then leave to cool.
2. Melt chocolate and butter together, whisk eggs and sugar together and temper into chocolate. Sift in flour.
3. Scatter half the Raspberries in the tart case and cover with the chocolate mix. Bake at 180‚Å∞C for 45 minutes until mix is set. Remove from the oven leave to cool slightly, portion and serve with cream and a few extra raspberries.
