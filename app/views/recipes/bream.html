Red Bream, Mediterranean Vegetables, Fried Foccacia and Edible Flowers


INGREDIENTS

1 Fillet red bream
Slice Foccacia
2 Orange Peppers
Selection Heritage Tomatoes
Basil
Edible Flowers
Olives
Olive oil

STEPS


1. Roast peppers and half the tomatoes in the oven with a little olive oil and salt.
2. Fry a slice of Foccacia in olive oil.
3. Cut raw tomatoes in half and remove stones from olives
4. Fry the bream on the skin side until crispy if not completely cooked turn and finish.
5. Arrange ingredients on plate finish with basil and edible flowers, Dress with olive oil.
